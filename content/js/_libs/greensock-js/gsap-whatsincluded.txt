This is a trimmed down package of GSAP libraries and plugins most commonly used:

	TweenMax (contains TimelineMax, basic plugins, easing)
	jquery.gsap.js (automatically hijacks jQuery .animate for better performance)
	scrollTo plugin (use for smooth scrolling instead of jQuery)

See the DOWNLOAD button here and choost CUSTOMIZE to see what else is normally included:
http://greensock.com/gsap
